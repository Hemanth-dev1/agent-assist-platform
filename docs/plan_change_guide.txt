PLAN CHANGE AND UPGRADE - SUPPORT KNOWLEDGE BASE

Q: Customer wants to upgrade from prepaid to postpaid.
A: 1) Check if number is eligible: active for 90+ days, no pending dues.
   2) Explain postpaid benefits: higher data, roaming included, monthly billing.
   3) Initiate migration via CRM > Plan > Migrate to Postpaid.
   4) Customer needs to submit ID proof at nearest store within 7 days.
   5) Migration completes within 24 hours of document verification.

Q: Customer wants to add an international roaming pack.
A: 1) Ask destination country and travel dates.
   2) Recommend correct pack: Asia Pack Rs.999/7days, Global Pack Rs.2499/15days.
   3) Activate via CRM > Add-ons > Roaming > Select Pack.
   4) Pack activates within 2 hours. Confirm via SMS.
   5) Remind customer to enable data roaming in phone settings.

Q: Customer wants to downgrade their plan.
A: 1) Note current plan and new requested plan.
   2) Warn about feature loss: reduced data, speed throttling after limit.
   3) Downgrade effective next billing cycle - cannot be same day.
   4) Process via CRM > Plan > Change Plan > Select lower tier.
   5) Send confirmation SMS with effective date.

Q: Customer asking about family plan or shared data.
A: 1) Family plan requires minimum 2 connections, maximum 6.
   2) Primary account holder manages all connections.
   3) Shared data pool: 100GB shared across all lines at Rs.1499/month.
   4) Initiate via CRM > Account > Family Plan > Add Members.
